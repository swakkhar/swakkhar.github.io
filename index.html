About

I am a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BRAC University. My research focuses on solving problems in computational intelligence and machine learning, with particular emphasis on applications in computational biology, bioinformatics, and related domains.

In teaching, my aim is to equip students with the practical skills and critical thinking needed to address real-world problems, fostering both technical competence and a strong sense of empathy.

Research Interests

  • Computational Biology and Bioinformatics
  • Information Retrieval
  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Understanding Multimodal Large Language Models
